Services

Mental Health Matters, LLC. provides assessment and treatment services via telehealth sessions to individuals and their families by a licensed mental health progessional through the use of evidence based strategies. It is the goal of Mental Health Matters, LLC to promote the well-being of others through accessible mental health services.


Areas of Specialty

  • Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)
  • Adjustment Disorders
  • Anxiety and Panic Disorders
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)
  • Borderline Personality Disorders
  • Depression
  • Problem Gambling
  • LGBTQIA Services
  • Oppositional Defiant Disorder (ODD)
  • Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Substance Abuse Disorder
